## About This Model

This articulated dragon is one of the most popular print-in-place designs. Each joint is carefully designed to move freely right off the print bed — no assembly, no supports, no glue needed.

## Print Settings

- **Layer Height:** 0.2mm
- **Infill:** 15%
- **Supports:** None
- **Raft:** No

## Tips

Make sure your first layer adhesion is dialed in. The joints are printed with a small gap, so any elephant's foot or first layer squish issues can cause the joints to fuse together.

Print slowly for the first few layers to ensure clean separation between the moving parts.
