## About This Model

This geometric planter features a low-poly aesthetic that looks great in any modern space. It comes with a matching drainage tray to protect your surfaces.

## Print Settings

- **Layer Height:** 0.2mm
- **Infill:** 20%
- **Supports:** None
- **Wall Count:** 3

## Notes

PETG is recommended for this model since it offers better water resistance than PLA. If you print in PLA, consider applying a food-safe sealant to the interior to prevent water damage over time.

The drainage holes are 5mm in diameter — large enough for proper drainage but small enough to retain soil.
