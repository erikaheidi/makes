## About This Model

This castle tower is designed for 28mm scale tabletop gaming. The roof is removable so you can place miniatures inside during gameplay. The walls feature detailed stone texturing.

## Print Settings

- **Layer Height:** 0.12mm (for best detail)
- **Infill:** 15%
- **Supports:** Yes (for window arches)
- **Scale:** 100% for 28mm scale

## Painting Tips

Prime with a grey spray primer, then dry-brush with lighter grey and white tones for the stone texture. Use a dark wash in the crevices to bring out the detail.

The removable roof can be painted separately for easier handling.
