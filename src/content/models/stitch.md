## About This Model

This adorable figurine recreates Stitch (Experiment 626) from Disney's beloved animated film *Lilo & Stitch*. The model captures the character's signature large ears, wide grin, and stocky build in great detail — perfect for display on your desk or shelf.

## Print Settings

- **Layer Height:** 0.1mm (for best detail)
- **Infill:** 15%
- **Supports:** Yes
- **Scale:** 100% recommended

## Tips

Use a light blue filament to get the most accurate color for Stitch. Printing at a lower layer height (0.1mm) will help capture the fine facial details and fur texture.

If printing in multiple parts, super glue works well for assembly. Sand and prime the model before painting for the best finish.
