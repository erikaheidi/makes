---
title: "Acrylic Basecoat and Seal"
description: "A reliable process for clean color coverage and durable finish."
---

1. Thin acrylic paint slightly and build color in multiple light passes to preserve detail.
2. Start with base colors, then add shading in recesses and highlights on edges.
3. Let the model dry completely before handling, especially between major color zones.
4. Finish with a clear coat: matte for display realism, satin for a mild sheen, gloss for vibrant, toy-like finishes.
